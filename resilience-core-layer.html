Resilience Core Layer

The innermost core is a refuge from digital technology—a fail-safe zone with accessible mechanical systems, independent life support, and operational headquarters. Basic, reliable, and self-sufficient when all else fails.

This is the heart of the Onion House—protected by all outer layers, operating independently when needed. No digital technology, only accessible mechanical systems you can see, touch, and repair. Direct exterior access ensures you can reach this space even when outer systems fail.

The core embodies system redundancy and resilience. It functions as both sanctuary and command center, comfortable yet utilitarian. Expandable below ground if needed, this space can support extended occupation with food production and life support capabilities—a rudimentary lab for survival and self-sufficiency. It represents the instinctive layer: reliable, comprehensible, and enduring.
