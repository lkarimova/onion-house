Onion House

The Onion House is a speculative dwelling where each spatial layer represents a different relationship between human life and its technological systems. Like the layers of an organism or a planet, each shell has its own sensory logic, autonomy level, and degree of technological exposure — forming a living interface between the digital, the mechanical, and the organic.
